Bio

Hello! My name is Kelbe Schneider and I am a old soul in a young body. I am one quarter away from graduating with a BA from the Visual Communication Design program at Eastern Washington University. I will also graduate with a minor in Art. Humanity is my religion and I follow The Law of the Jungle. I believe in equality. I have always had a love for animals and was raised as a vegetarian. I have a desire to learn about any topic I can get my hands on. I have the ability to adjust and feel at home most any place I enter or travel to, near or far. I am a lover of the outdoors and adventures. I am big on rock climbing. No adventure is too big or too small to take on in my eyes.

Professional Objectives

I want to pursue a job where I learn something new everyday. I am unsure of an actual title I would like to pursue. However, I would like to work and grow in my field or outside my field even for a few years before going back to school to earn my Masters. Long term, I think I want to teach or move to another country to pursue design on a higher level. Potential companies I want to work for include, but are not limited to: Arm the Animals, Nike, Prana, Disney, Viking River Cruises, and Connections from Hallmark.

“To live & travel would be an awfully big adventure.”
